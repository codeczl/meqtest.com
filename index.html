About MEQ Test

The Morningness-Eveningness Questionnaire (MEQ) is a scientific assessment tool designed to determine your chronotype. Understanding whether you're a morning type, evening type, or somewhere in between can help you optimize your schedule and improve your quality of life.

This Test Helps You:

  • Identify your peak performance hours
  • Optimize your sleep schedule
  • Enhance daily productivity
  • Improve your overall well-being
